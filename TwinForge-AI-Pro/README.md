## Overview

TwinForge AI is an Industry 4.0 platform that combines IoT, Artificial Intelligence, and Digital Twin technology to monitor industrial machines, predict failures before they occur, and provide intelligent maintenance recommendations.

The platform creates a real-time digital representation of physical machines while continuously analyzing sensor data using AI.

---

## Features

- Predictive Maintenance
- Digital Twin Visualization
- Real-Time Sensor Monitoring
- AI Failure Prediction
- Remaining Useful Life (RUL)
- Explainable AI
- Factory Dashboard
- Maintenance Recommendations
- Historical Data Analysis
- Industrial Reports
