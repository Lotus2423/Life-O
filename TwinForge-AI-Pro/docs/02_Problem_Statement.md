# Problem Statement

## Current Situation

Most factories still rely on:

- Reactive Maintenance (repair after failure)
- Preventive Maintenance (fixed schedule)

These methods often fail to predict unexpected machine breakdowns.

---

## Problems

- Unexpected production downtime
- High maintenance cost
- Loss of productivity
- Human error during inspection
- Poor machine health visibility
- Expensive enterprise solutions

---

## Proposed Solution

TwinForge AI combines IoT sensors, Artificial Intelligence, and Digital Twin technology to monitor machine health continuously.

The system predicts possible failures before they occur and recommends maintenance actions.
