# Vision

TwinForge AI is an AI-powered Industry 4.0 platform that creates a real-time Digital Twin of industrial machines and predicts failures before they occur.

The platform aims to reduce machine downtime, improve maintenance planning, and provide intelligent recommendations using IoT, Artificial Intelligence, and Digital Twin technology.

---

# Mission

To build an affordable predictive maintenance platform that can be used by industries of all sizes.

---

# Goals

- Predict machine failures before breakdown
- Reduce maintenance costs
- Reduce unexpected downtime
- Increase machine lifespan
- Improve production efficiency
- Assist maintenance engineers with AI recommendations

---

# Target Users

- Manufacturing Industries
- Small & Medium Enterprises (SMEs)
- Maintenance Engineers
- Production Managers
- Industrial Automation Companies
- Engineering Students
