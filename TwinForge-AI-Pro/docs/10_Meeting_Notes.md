## Discussion

Different Industry 4.0 project ideas were compared.

Evaluated ideas:

- Predictive Maintenance
- AI Quality Inspection
- Tool Wear Monitoring
- Smart Energy Monitoring
- Digital Twin for Manufacturing Cell

After evaluation, it was decided to combine Predictive Maintenance with Digital Twin technology into one integrated platform.

## Final Decision

Project Name

Industry 4.0

Artificial Intelligence

IoT

Digital Twin

## Problem Statement

Factories often experience unexpected machine failures.

Current maintenance methods are:

- Reactive Maintenance
- Preventive Maintenance

Both methods have limitations.

The project will focus on Predictive Maintenance.

---

## Selected Machine

Three Phase Induction Motor

Reason:

- Common in industries
- Easy to monitor
- Suitable for AI prediction
- Easy to prototype
